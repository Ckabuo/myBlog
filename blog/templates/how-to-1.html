How to extract text from a picture using Bixby vision

Gather around Samsung users, this one is for you.i'm sure you know about Samsungs Bixby Assistant, but do you know its feautures?, one of which is Bixby Vision.
So what is bixby Vision?
Bixby Vision is an intelligent camera feature that is built into Samsung devices, allowing you to extract text from a picture with just a few taps. Here's how you can extract text from a picture using Bixby Vision:

Step 1
Open the camera app:
To use Bixby Vision, you'll first need to open the camera app on your Samsung device.

Step 2
Activate Bixby Vision:
Once you have opened the camera app, swipe to the right to access Bixby Vision. You should see a Bixby Vision logo on the bottom of the screen.

Step 3
Choose the "Text" option:
Once you have activated Bixby Vision, tap on the "Text" option to extract text from a picture. You can also select this option by swiping up on the screen.

Step 4
Take a picture or select a photo:
Next, you'll need to take a picture of the text you want to extract, or select a photo from your gallery. Bixby Vision will automatically identify and extract the text from the image.

Step 5
Copy the text:
Once the text has been extracted, you'll see it displayed on the screen. To copy the text, simply tap on it and select the "Copy" option.

Step 6
Use the extracted text:
You can now use the extracted text for various purposes, such as searching the web, creating a new note, or pasting it into another app.

Another nice car that can be purchased in Nigeria for two million Naira is the Hyundai Sonata from 2004 to 2008. It still looks great and works well despite being an older version. The main problem is that you may not see it for sale, due to its scarcity in Nigeria.

You'll be shocked to find out that you can easily buy an eighth-generation Honda Civic (2005-2010) for two million Naira. At that cost, it's a great deal. It has a small steering wheel with good road traction and good fuel efficiency.

The TSX is Honda’s compact executive car sold through its Acura division, yes, you read that right, Acura is owned by Honda, so you should expect Honda reliability with the TSX but in a more luxury way. The TSX was built by Honda (Acura) to compete with BMW 3series and Mercedes C class. With just 2 million Naira you can buy yourself a TSX.

1. LEXUS ES330

The number 1 car on this list that you can get for two million Naira apart from the corolla is the Lexus ES330 (fourth gen). The Lexus brand is actually growing more popular than Benz and BMW As a result of customers learning that Lexus is a luxury car brand that is reliable and inexpensive despite not costing a lot of money.

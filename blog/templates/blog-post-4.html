WHY THIS KIA RIO IS THE BEST FIRST CAR FOR EVERY YOUNG WORKING NIGERIAN

Currently, I own the one vehicle that properly represents the Nigerian working class of between the ages of 24 and 32 (I'm talking about the cooperate people); this vehicle embodies all that. after spending quality time with it, I can give you three reasons with certainty why this car is one of the best cars, if not the best car, for a Nigerian who is just starting out in life and searching for a daily driver.
I would explain why the Kia Rio is effective for any young Nigerian who wants to get up every morning and drive a car to work and back. Three reasons. I’d also let you know about the challenges you'll face and the cost of maintaining and owning a Kia Rio in Nigeria.

1. IRRESTIBLE APPEAL (APPEALLING EXTERIOR)

The piano black grille (Tiger Nose grille) on this car will catch your eye right away. At contrast to most cars, which have mesh-like grilles with air vents in the front, this one has holes but you can't see them. When Kia decided to give the Rio an electric car feeling, it became one of the key features that gives this car a very bold appearance. The Rio is also not particularly luxurious, but it has presence; when you see one on the road, you will know that someone with serious intentions is moving, and you can't help but give it a second glance. Back in the day, the Kia Rio used to be quite little, but today it rivals the Toyota Corolla in terms of dimensions.and also,the Rio rides on 15-inch rims.

2. ENTERTAINING INTERIOR

The Rio's interior, which has a fun vibe, is the second factor that helps it function. The steering is the first inside feature you'll notice; it's somewhat elegant, bears the kia emblem, and contains buttons for the infotainment system. The entertainment system has a contemporary style and the interface is very straightforward. The car has both Apple Car Play and Android Auto, allowing you to connect with your Android or Apple smartphone. Since the Rio is not a luxury vehicle, it has cloth seats, which are also comfy to sit in. The Rio's trunk capacity is also sizable enough to fit a trip's worth of bags.
Sincerity be told, most Nigerians, especially those between the ages of 24 and 32, don't really care what kind of engine an automobile has; they just want something fashionable, fun and useful.

3. VERY POCKET FRIENDLY

The Rio is unique for people just starting out in life because, in general, it is quite economical and the engine is cheap to run. Every Kia Rio is built with a 1.6-liter direct-injected 4-cylinder engine that produces 130 horsepower and a 6-speed manual or automatic transmission with sport mode. The Rio runs well and has a very silent startup sound.
The Rio is an ideal car for someone just starting out life, it is economical to own and run, it is cheap and the maintenance is low.
I have given you three reasons why the Kia Rio is the best if you are just starting out life, now let’s talk about the problems and cost of maintenance in Nigeria

COST AND PROBLEMS IN NIGERIA

1. VERY CLOSE TO THE GROUND

The Rio's stance is its first problem; it is very low to the ground, and on rough or bad roads, the front lip constantly scrapes the ground.

2. INFOTAINMENT SCREN DRIES UP WITH TIME (For some model)

The second issue is that with time, the infotainment screen on some models begins to bleed out from the center. You are aware of how a fallen calculator screen spills ink

3. THROTTLE FAILS TO ACCELERATE

The majority of Rio owners have observed that the car develops an electrical problem with the throttle body after two to three years of ownership, making it impossible for the car to accelerate over 40 km/h. However, since it can be fixed, taking it in for service will not spell the end of the world. The mechanics will simply need to replace a few parts.

4. THE TRUNK RELEASE BUTTON MIGHT FAIL

The final issue is that trunk release button's ability to function may be compromised by a loose cable or wear from use. It is, however, a straightforward repair.

Now that you have seen the reasons why the Kia Rio might be the best car just for you, as well as the problems that comes with it, you might be asking; How much can I snatch up this bad boy for?. I’m going to reveal that to you in a bit.

COST

You may be able to purchase a Tokunbo (Foreign Used) 2016, 2017ia Rio, which is the 4th generation and most recent model of the Rio, for between 3,500,000 and 4,700,000.
You must keep up with normal vehicle maintenance and servicing if you want to enjoy driving your Rio. This will help your car live longer and run well for you.
If you want something different, a breath of fresh air, instead of a Toyota or a Honda, the Kia Rio is a vehicle that functions as an everyday vehicle.
